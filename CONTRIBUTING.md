# Contributing to EcoPulse AI

First off, thank you for considering contributing to **EcoPulse AI**! It's people like you who help make this professional-grade environmental intelligence system even better.

3. **Make your changes**: Ensure your code follows the established [Code Style](#code-style).

5. **Submit a Pull Request**: Provide a detailed description of your changes.
